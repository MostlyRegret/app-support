# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Oil Tracker / Maintenance Tracker does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.

## Data Storage

All data created in the app is stored **locally on your device only**.

This includes:

- Vehicles
- Service records
- Maintenance schedules
- Notes
- Photos or attachments (if used)

The app does not send this information to any external servers.

## Internet Access

The app does **not require an internet connection** to function.

## Analytics and Tracking

This app does **not use analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.**

## Notifications

The app may schedule **local notifications** on your device to remind you of maintenance intervals.

These notifications are generated locally and are **not sent from a server.**

## Data Sharing

The app does **not share any data with third parties.**

## User Control

All data remains on the user's device and can be deleted by:

- Removing records inside the app
- Uninstalling the application
